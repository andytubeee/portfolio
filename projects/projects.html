Hackathon Projects


  • Tree Companion

    Meet your companion, who will be there with you as you go on environmental adventures and complete tasks to help the earth. Not only will you have a companion, but you will be rewarded for your good karma. This app is powered through crowdsourcing where users can post environmental issues on the map related to pollution, wildlife, natural resources or in general any environmental problem the area is facing! In the app, you can engage in friendly competition and compete against other people to see who can earn the most points! These points can be converted to cryptocurrencies which can be used to buy new companions to embark on the journey with you, buy premium rewards, or gift points to friends! Not only does this app support social wellbeing amongst the community, but it also motivates people to help clean the earth!
